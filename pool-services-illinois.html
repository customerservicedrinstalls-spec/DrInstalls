Illinois Pool Service FAQs

Common questions from Illinois homeowners

In Illinois, the ideal window for pool opening is late April through mid-May. You want to wait until nighttime temperatures are consistently above 50°F, which usually happens in the Chicagoland area around early May. Opening too early risks algae blooms from fluctuating spring temperatures, while waiting past Memorial Day means losing valuable swim time. We start booking Illinois openings in March, so plan ahead to secure your preferred date.

Illinois pool owners should plan to close between mid-September and late October. The sweet spot is when water temperatures drop below 65°F consistently — typically early to mid-October in the Chicago suburbs. Given Illinois' tendency for sudden cold snaps, we strongly recommend not waiting past the end of October. A late freeze can cause serious damage to exposed plumbing and equipment. Book your closing by early September to guarantee your spot.
