Indiana Pool Service FAQs

Answers for Northwest Indiana pool owners

Northwest Indiana pool owners should target late April to mid-May for opening. Lake-effect weather from Lake Michigan can keep temperatures cooler in communities like Munster and Hobart compared to areas further south, so it's wise to wait until consistent warmth arrives. We generally recommend waiting until nighttime temps hold above 50°F for at least a week. Our Indiana bookings fill up quickly in spring, so we suggest scheduling by late March.

Lake Michigan's influence means Northwest Indiana can see earlier and heavier snowfall than areas further inland. Communities in Lake County — Schererville, St. John, Dyer — can experience sudden cold snaps as early as mid-October. We recommend scheduling your pool closing for late September to mid-October in the Region. Don't wait for November — an early freeze can crack pipes and damage your pump system. Better safe than sorry with lake-effect weather in play.
